Delete your Trivia Dash account

Last updated: January 2026

This page explains how to delete your Trivia Dash account (package com.lizzi.triviadash), what data is removed, and what is kept.

What gets deleted

  • Your Supabase profile (display name, avatar, account identifier, email link)
  • Your cloud-synced gameplay data (scores, Elo, match history, leaderboard entries)
  • Any party-lobby records linked to your account

What we keep

  • Anonymised aggregate statistics (these cannot be linked back to you)
  • Billing records held by Google Play — these are managed by Google and retained for tax and legal reasons
  • Limited records we are legally required to keep (for example, fraud-prevention logs), kept only as long as the law requires

Option 1 — In the app

  1. Open Trivia Dash.
  2. Go to Settings → Account and tap Delete account (if available in your app version).
  3. If you only want to remove local data: Android Settings → Apps → Trivia Dash → Storage → Clear data, then uninstall.

Option 2 — Request cloud deletion by email

Email support@triviadash.app from the address linked to your Google account, with the subject Delete my Trivia Dash account. Please include the display name you used in the app so we can identify your record.

We will confirm receipt within 72 hours and complete deletion of your Supabase profile and online stats within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation once it is done.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not cancel a Trivia Dash Pro subscription. Cancel it separately in Google Play → Subscriptions. Cancellation stops future renewals; access to Pro continues until the end of the period already paid for.

Need help?

If you can’t access the email linked to your account, or you have any other questions, contact support@triviadash.app and we’ll help verify your identity another way.
